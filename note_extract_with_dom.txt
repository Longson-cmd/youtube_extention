Production Risks (DOM Transcript Extraction)

1. YouTube DOM/classes are unstable
- Risk: Selector-based extraction is brittle and may break when YouTube updates markup.
- Impact: Transcript reads can fail without code changes on our side.

2. Text-keyword matching is locale/experiment sensitive
- Risk: Control detection based on UI text can break across languages or A/B tests.
- Impact: Script may fail to find/open transcript button or menu item.

3. Fixed retry/sleep timing is flaky
- Risk: Static delays can be too short on slow pages or unnecessary on fast pages.
- Impact: Intermittent failures and inconsistent extraction behavior.
